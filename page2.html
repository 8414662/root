Consumer Products

Warner Bros. Consumer Products extends the Studio’s portfolio of entertainment into the lives of fans around the blobe. WBCP partners with globally on award-winning ranges of toys, fashion and publishing inspired by franchises such as DC, J.K. Rowling’s Wizarding World and Looney Tunes.

The division’s successful global entertainment business includes groundbreaking experiences like The Wizarding World of Harry Potter in Orlando, FL, Osaka, Japan and Hollywood, which opened in June 2018. With innovative global licensing and merchandising programs, promotional partnerships and themed experiences, WBCP is the leading licensing and retail merchandising organization in the globe.
