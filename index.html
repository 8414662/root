We are a motion pictures and entertainment company based in Burbank, California.

Warner Brothers. Pictures is a global leader in marketing and distribution of feature films. operating offices in more than 20 countries and releasing films in 120 international territories, either directly to theatres or in conjunction with partner companies and co-ventures.

Warner Bros. Animation is one of the best leading producers of animation in entertainment, with innovative and talent-rich rosters boasting some of the most accomplished writers, producers and artists today. WBA is home to the iconic animated characters from DC and Looney Tunes libraries. The studio is the cutting edge of animation technology, and has both CG and traditionally animated projects in production and development. WBA also creates highly successful series of DC original animated movies for the DVD.
